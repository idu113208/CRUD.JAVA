## 2024-05-22 - [Filter Inputs Accessibility]
**Learning:** Compact horizontal forms often neglect labels, relying on placeholders. This fails WCAG 3.3.2.
**Action:** Always add `.visually-hidden` labels to filter inputs if visible labels break the layout.
